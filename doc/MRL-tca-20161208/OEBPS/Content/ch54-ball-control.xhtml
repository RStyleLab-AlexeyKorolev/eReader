BALL CONTROL

  • Using all surfaces of the feet and body from all distances on the field
  • Receiving the ball at the highest level of speed with the inside or outside of both feet away from pressure
  • Ability to turn with physical pressure with either foot inside or outside to beat opponent
  • Ability to control the ball out of the air at full speed from 50 yards plus with both feet, either thigh, or on the chest
  • Receiving to turn with the ball with physical pressure out of the air or off the bounce

LIVING YOUR SPORT

At Level 3, players who live the sport of soccer begin studying professional players, especially those that play the positions they want to play. This activity will continue at Level 4, as the student-athlete becomes even more of a student of the game and an enthusiastic fan.

One professional recruiter told the story of a student-athlete who, when asked about her favorite player, talked about a lesser-known player in glowing terms. The recruiter immediately recognized someone who was a true student of the game.

TECHNICAL TIPS >>

  • Assess the flight of the ball
  • Time the jump to head the ball at the highest point
  • Keep eyes open
  • Lock the neck and arch the back to snap through the ball with the head
  • Thrust from the waist
  • Use a technical snap to create power

COMMON MISTAKES >>

  • Closing eyes prior to contact
  • Mistiming the jump
  • Mistiming the technical snap of the head
  • Positioning the feet incorrectly, causing poor balance

PASSING

  • Ability to weight the ball in to feet or lead them
  • Driving balls with ranges of 40 to 50 yards
  • Bending the ball to switch the field or cross in the final third
  • Chipping the ball with the right weight and timing with teammate
  • Using the outside of the foot for deception and to release a quick outlet
  • Heel and flick passing to find creative movement off of opponents

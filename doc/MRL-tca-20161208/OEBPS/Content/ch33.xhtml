3.3 FITNESS

FITNESS matters. Strength and speed are required to play the game of soccer effectively. Flexibility and mobility help to prevent injuries. As a youth soccer player reaches Level 3, she can separate herself from other players by demonstrating a serious dedication to her own personal fitness. Not only does this help to establish a foundation of strength; it also teaches good habits needed for success in higher levels of soccer.

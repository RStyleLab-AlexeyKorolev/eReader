PROFESSIONALISM

As a youth soccer player becomes a student-athlete, professionalism takes on a new meaning. She now plays for a college team, and she needs to represent that team with dignity and pride—both on and off the field, as well as on- and offline. Not doing so can have serious consequences in terms of any scholarships she may have received.

A student-athlete should always maintain professionalism in terms of her appearance, her attitude, and her actions. Some examples include:

  • Learning rules of etiquette and always displaying good manners
  • Dressing appropriately off the field—no obscene T-shirts, no skimpy clothing, etc.
  • Wearing team apparel that represents her college or university, not another one
  • Keeping her online presence uncontroversial
  • Staying away from inappropriate situations, such as parties where drugs may be present
  • Avoiding the bar scene, especially if she is underage

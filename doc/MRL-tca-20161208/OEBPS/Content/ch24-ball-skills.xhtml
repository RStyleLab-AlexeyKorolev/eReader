FOUNDATIONAL BALL SKILLS/DRIBBLING

  • All surfaces of the feet
  • Change of direction
  • Change of pace
  • Taking players on
  • Keeping possession
  • Creative risk-taking

TECHNICAL TIPS >>

  • Master the 4 cuts with inside and outside of both feet
  • Maintain control of the ball
  • Manipulate the ball
  • Control speed with the ball
  • Change speed after the cut
  • Timing your cut

COMMON MISTAKES >>

  • Losing control of the ball
  • Doing too many cuts instead of cutting and dribbling
  • Timing the cut wrong
  • Losing control of the ball before or after the cut
  • Not using both feet

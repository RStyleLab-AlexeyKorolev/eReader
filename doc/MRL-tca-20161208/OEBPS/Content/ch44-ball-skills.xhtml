FOUNDATIONAL BALL SKILLS/DRIBBLING

  • Improving your moves to 7-8, with emphasis on using both feet to execute
  • Using your speed/pace with the ball at the right moments, because most opponents will have similar athletic abilities
  • Being aggressive in the right areas to read the covering defender
  • Deception with upper and lower body to put opponent off-balance
  • Ability to use creative skill to pick the best side to get in behind opponents

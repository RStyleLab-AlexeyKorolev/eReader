4.4 TECHNIQUE

Level 4 is a very high level of soccer requiring the importance of shifts to the ability to perform in press-ured situations during games. Remember that a COMPLETE ATHLETE not only masters the basic and advanced skills needed to play the game of soccer, but she also understands the roles and responsibilities of every position on a team and how all the positions work together to win games. Moving into the collegiate level, all the skills that you learned in Levels 1, 2, and 3 need to be used at an even higher level of efficiency, speed, and thinking in orderto perform in big-game environments.

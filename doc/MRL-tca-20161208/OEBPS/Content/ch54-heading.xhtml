HEADING

  • For distance, passing or clearing over the opponents’ midfield
  • Flicking with timing of teammates’ run
  • Clearing high and wide to stop pressure
  • Deceptive movement and timing to step in front or behind to lose opponent and then to snap flick across the goalkeeper to finish, or head down either side of the goalkeeper to finish
  • Diving header waist-height ball at speed to clear the ball, or arrive in the area at full speed to get on the end of wide service to finish

FOUNDATIONAL BALL SKILLS/ DRIBBLING

  • Consistent dribbling habits on both feet to keep
  • all abilities sharp and consistent
  • Ability to read opponents’ weaknesses to use your moves effectively
  • Being able to perform with creative skill, speed, and change of pace with both feet, and to play either side of the field or the center
  • Deception to freeze opponents with upper and lower body
  • Ability to read teammates’ movement, in order to penetrate opponents’ open areas
